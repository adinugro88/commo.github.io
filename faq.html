I’ve selected one of the plans, what’s the next step?

Your dedicated personal project manager will be assigned to you and contact you via WhatsApp, Text or Email. Your project manager would guide you further and you will be able to start the project right away.

How many designs to be requested at one time?

You can submit as many design requests as you want. Depending on your plan, either one or more of your projects will be worked on at the same time.

How long do revisions take?

Revisions are completed within 24 hours.

I have a multi-businesses. Can I use 1 subscription plan?

Yes, it is depending on your subscription plan.You are allowed to request and create design projects for your multi-businesses and brands. You can use Commo for yourself, your other businesses, or even your customers.

Are Commo designers experienced?

Commo designers have a wide variety of skill sets and they’re ready to apply it to your project requests. Whether you need designs for your social media or custom illustrations for t-shirts, our awesome team of designers can create anything you need.
